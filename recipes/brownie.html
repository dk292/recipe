Brownie Cookies

Ingredients

  1. 4 ounces baking chocolate (unsweetened or bittersweet)
  2. 2 cups granulated sugar
  3. 2 sticks salted butter, softened
  4. 3 large eggs
  5. 1 tablespoon vanilla extractr
  6. 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  7. 1/4 cup plus 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
  8. 1 teaspoon baking powder
  9. 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  10. Powdered sugar, for dusting
